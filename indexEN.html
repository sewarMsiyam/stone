Who Are We

We Represent The Stone And Marble Industry In Palestine With More Than 800 Members

The Federation of Stone and Marble Industry was established in Palestine in 1996 AD as an independent, non-governmental, non-profit organization based on membership and with the aim of defending the interests of members and working to promote and develop the Palestinian stone industry, noting that this industry represents about 4.5% of the gross domestic product and provides job opportunities with more than 20,000 workers, and as a true representative of this industry, the union includes in its membership more than 800 companies representing the majority of stone and marble establishments in the West Bank, and provides

The Stone Industry Federation, Under The Supervision Of The Ministry Of National Economy And In Cooperation With UNIDO, Begins The Executive Phase... Specialized In The Stone Industry Sector To Implement Solutions That Support The Cost Of Electric Energy Through Alternative Energy

The Union Of Stone And Marble Industries - Nablus Branch Held The Day Before Yesterday An Expanded Workshop In The Presence Of More Than 25 Stone And Marble Companies At The Union's Headquarters Located In Hawara - Nablus, In Partnership With The Ministry Of National Economy And The United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO), With The Aim Of Clarifying A Sustainable Project. For Solar Energy And All The Services Provided By The Project. The Meeting Was Attended By The Head Of The Sub-Commission In Nablus, Mr. Ayed Odeh, The Treasurer Of The Union In Palestine, Mr. Abdel Qader Makhlouf, The Members Of The Sub-Administrative Body, Messrs. Nazih Najm And Abdel-Rahman Salameh, The General Manager Of The Union In Palestine, Mr. Shadi Shaheen, And The Branch Manager In Nablus Mr. Malik Jabr, A Representative Of The Ministry Of National Economy, Nablus, Eng. Adnan Abu Haniyeh, And A Number Of Members Of The Federation's General Authority In Nablus.
